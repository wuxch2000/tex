Embedded Software Engineer

RBR Ltd. - Kanata, Ottawa, Canada (Ottawa, Canada Area)

Job Description

Come make a splash!  Scientific instruments that make the measurements of the state of the planet's oceans need the utmost in performance, stability, and elegant design.  As part of our multi-displinary engineering team, your firmware will be at the heart of our products.

 

RBR is developing a new line of scientific data loggers that can be deployed at the bottom of the ocean. The component pieces of firmware, hardware, and software combine to give the highest levels of measurement accuracy with ease of use that is unsurpassed.

 

We are looking for motivated and collaborative firmware developers with more than five years experience to join our collaborative and collegial team.  Specifically:

  • Developing software for low-power microcontrollers running an RTOS
  • Comment on digital designs for sensor interfacing
  • Design signal processing code for FFTs and other algorithms
  • Make intelligent conversation around the coffee machine

 

We expect you will be up and running immediately.

 

Desired Skills & Experience

You should have five years of deep C/C++ skills, with embedded processors and RTOSs at the forefront of that experience.  Reading a digital schematic is a useful skill, as is the ability to do some quick hack code on a PC to analyse data or signals (Python? Matlab?).  You will have used version control systems (Subversion, Git, Hg) and have worked in a team where issue tracking was important (Jira).  Your father should not smell of elderberries.  Excellent reading and writing skills, and the ability to remember what was strange about this ad in the interview.

 

Company Description

RBR makes submersible data loggers for the blue planet. From the simplest of temperature recorders, to the most complex multi-parameter sondes, our instruments are used on every continent of the globe to measurement the smallest of signals - indicators of climate changes, oil leaks, and ocean circulation.

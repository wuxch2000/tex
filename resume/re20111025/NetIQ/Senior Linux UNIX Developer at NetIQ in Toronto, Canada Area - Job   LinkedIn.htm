Senior Linux/UNIX Developer

NetIQ - Toronto, Canada Area

Job Description

Senior Linux/UNIX Developer

Downtown Toronto, ON

 

NetIQ (formerly PlateSpin/Novell) is seeking experienced aSenior Linux/UNIX Developer to assume key creative roles in developing ourindustry leading datacenter management and planning products.  We are looking for people with the rightblend of skills, education, experience, and attitude to fill this exciting andchallenging position. We offer a top tier work environment, benefits and allyou would expect from a market leading software company that is now among thetop 12 largest in the world.

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Design and develop software products related toPlateSpin’s Virtualization Technology, Datacenter Optimization, and ServicesManagement Tools.
  • Implement appropriate unit and system leveltests to ensure high quality code prior to hand-off to Test Engineering.
  • Mentor junior members of the team.
  • Research new technologies as they relate toPlateSpin’s product offerings.
  • Work closely with the Product Management,Support, and Test Engineering teams to achieve the highest business value forthe company.
  • Have fun while doing whatever it takes to getthe job done.

 

Skills:

 

  • At least 5 years of demonstrated professionalexperience in the following:
  • C/C++
  • OOA/OOD/OOP
  • Distributed systems
  • Developing multi-process, multi-threadedapplications
  • Strong understanding of the Linux kernel anddevice drivers
  • Linux system-level programming using sockets,IPC, and threads
  • Experience with the installation andconfiguration of various Linux distros
  • Experience with scripting languages (bash, perl,python)
  • Strong analytical, research, and problem-solvingskills
  • Strong interpersonal, verbal and writtencommunication skills
  • Understanding and recognition of Design Patterns

 

The following skills are desirable:

 

  • Programming knowledge of Windows and UNIXoperating system flavors
  • Unit Testing Frameworks (NUnit, CPPUnit, JUnit)
  • Knowledge of distributed protocols
  • SOAP, RMI, JMS, IPC, .Net Remoting, DCOM
  • Knowledge of Virtualization Technologies
  • Virtual Machines, VMware, Xen, Microsoft VirtualServer
  • Knowledge of XML, XSD, XSL
  • Knowledge of Data Center components and theiruses and interactions
  • SANs, Switches, Routers, Blades
  • Knowledge of database design
  • Table normalization, efficient SQL queries,performance tuning
  • Knowledge of system performance paradigms
  • Load balancing, clusters, distributed execution,agents
  • Knowledge of formal software developmentmethodologies
  • Use of source control tools such as CVS andSubversion
  • Working knowledge of SqlServer, MySql, orPostgreSQL, Hibernate
  • Knowledge of algorithm design
  • NP approximations, optimization, workflow

 

Qualifications:

 

  • A university degree in Computer Science,Computer Engineering, or equivalent.
  • A minimum of 5 years software developmentexperience in a commercial software development environment.

Company Description

NetIQ helps organizations cost-effectively tackle information protection challenges and manage the complexity of dynamic, highly-distributed application environments. NetIQ's scalable, automated management solutions for Security & Compliance, Identity & Access, and Performance & Availability help customers realize demonstrable business improvement and IT cost savings. NetIQ's parent company, the Attachmate Group, recently acquired Novell and merged the Identity & Security and Data Center products of Novell into the NetIQ portfolio. With this expanded product offering, NetIQ can provide a full suite of solutions to address the security and data center management needs of the enterprise.

Senior Software Developer - BlackBerry Messenger

Research In Motion - Mississauga, CA-ON (Canada)

Job Description

Research In Motion Limited® (RIM)® is a world leader in the mobile communications market and has a history of developing breakthrough wireless solutions. RIM's portfolio of award-winning products, services and embedded technologies is used by thousands of organizations around the world and includes the BlackBerry® wireless platform, the RIM Wireless Handheld™ product line, software development tools and software/hardware licensing agreements. RIM is seeking driven individuals who can take our wireless data products to the next level in the global wireless market. Are you ready to make a difference in the world of mobile communications with RIM?

POSITION SUMMARY

Want to develop on cutting edge BlackBerry Servers supporting millions of users around the world? Want to design and implement new and upcoming BlackBerry features? Want to work in a dynamic and fun team? This is the job for you.

As a key member of the server development team responsible for BlackBerry Messenger server solutions, the successful candidate will be responsible for the design and development of high quality, reliable, sustainable, and high-performance software at the core of the Blackberry Messenger service. Duties will include maintenance of an existing Java server codebase, and research and development for the next-generation Blackberry Messenger server.

ESSENTIAL SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS
  • University degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or equivalent
  • Excellent programming skills and the ability to learn quickly
  • Expert-level knowledge of C++ or Java and an understanding of Object-Oriented architecture and design
  • Experience writing client- and server-side code, creating high-volume production applications, and developing prototypes quickly
  • Expertise in building platforms and networking infrastructure
  • A strong background in distributed systems, including development experience in OS/kernel, network system, or large scale storage
  • Experience with TCP/IP and network programming
  • Experience developing multi-threaded applications or distributed servers

ADDITIONAL ASSETS
  • Knowledge of the proper application of design patterns
  • Familiarity with distributed hash tables, SQL database development, or large scale storage system.
  • Familiarity with Linux/UNIX systems

If you're driven to take wireless technologies to the next level, it's time you join the team at RIM. We offer a challenging environment that fosters creativity and rewards excellence. Employees also have use of our award winning BlackBerry!
